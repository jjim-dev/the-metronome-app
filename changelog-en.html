Application evolution and improvements

v1.1.0 15 Jun 2026
  • Ability to disable ads for one hour in the free version.
  • New general menu with more accessible options.
  • Beat options: BPM, Half Note, Quarter Note, Eighth Note, Dotted Half Note, Dotted Quarter Note, Dotted Eighth Note.
  • Hourglass icon in the bars counter to highlight the waiting time.
  • Improved backup management. Added option to delete cloud content and enhanced security with confirmation dialogs for all user actions in this section.
  • Metronome engine with improved accuracy and stability.

Official launch of The Metronome on the Google Play Store!
